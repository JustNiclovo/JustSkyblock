Skyblock

Skyblock is a fun MMO RPG recreating the og minecraft feeling but with a lot of addons

You start the game on your private island. this is a place privatly for you to use to place storage or minions or anything you want.

After exploring your private island you can go to the hub, a common place where you can meet other players, talk to different merchants and do a lot of fun activities like farming, fishing, foraging and killing zombies.
